"TEENAGERS"

Teenagers often lead busy lives filled with school, extracurricular activities, and social responsibilities. Engaging in relaxing activities can be beneficial for their mental and emotional well-being.

mind-relaxing activities that teenagers can try:

1.Encourage teenagers to pick up a good book, whether it's fiction, non-fiction, or even graphic novels.

2.Teach them basic meditation and mindfulness techniques to help reduce stress and increase self-awareness.

3. Painting, drawing, writing, and other creative activities can be very relaxing. It allows them to express themselves and tap into their imagination.

4.Spending time in nature can have a calming effect. Encourage them to go for a walk in the park, hike in the woods, or just sit in a garden to connect with the outdoors.

Preparing a favorite dish or trying out a new recipe can be a therapeutic and rewarding activity.
